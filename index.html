COVID-19 Statistics

ABOUT

We are an organization that provides open sources of crime pattern analysis. The app tracks down the number of car accidents due to alcohol consumption across the levels of COVID-19. We believe that our statics are valid and have been collected by professional personal across the Western Cape province.
All around the province criminologist have used our statics to study them, compare from neighbourhood to neighbourhood, and compile new laws, restrictions to abide by during the COVID-19 levels. Criminologists have further used our statistic to prevent vehicle accidents from happening due to alcohol in take.
